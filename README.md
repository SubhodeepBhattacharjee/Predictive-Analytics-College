## ðŸ“Š Feature Description

### CGPA
- Overall academic performance of the student.

### Internships
- Number of internships completed.
- Reflects industry exposure and practical experience.

### Projects
- Number of academic or personal projects completed.
- Indicates hands-on technical skills.

### Workshops / Certifications
- Number of workshops or certifications completed.
- Shows the student's initiative to upskill through online or offline courses.

### AptitudeTestScore
- Score obtained in aptitude tests.
- Measures quantitative ability and logical reasoning used in recruitment processes.

### SoftSkillRating
- Rating of communication and interpersonal skills.
- Plays a crucial role in interviews and workplace success.

### ExtraCurricularActivities
- Participation level in extracurricular activities.
- Provides insight into personality, leadership, and adaptability.

### PlacementTraining
- Indicates whether the student has undergone placement training.
- Helps prepare for aptitude tests, interviews, and group discussions.

### SSC
- Marks obtained in Secondary School Certificate (Class 10).
- Represents early academic foundation.

### HSC
- Marks obtained in Higher Secondary Certificate (Class 12).
- Reflects academic preparedness before higher education.
